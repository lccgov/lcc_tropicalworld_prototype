• What is Safeguarding Adults?

    Safeguarding adults refers to the multi-agency procedure used to protect adults from abuse or neglect.

    The procedures help people aged 18 years of age or over, with care and support needs.

    For more information

  • What is the Safeguarding Adults Board?

    The Leeds Safeguarding Adults Board is a statutory body, made up of organisations that work together to safeguard adults from abuse and neglect.

    The achievements of the Board are set out in its Annual Reports.
